Capturing Glacial Debris Flows on Mt. Rainier

Mt. Rainier National Park, WA - July, 2021

 

Working with a team from the USGS Cascades Volcano Observatory, I helped carry and deploy Farifield Seismic Nodes along the outwash plain of the Tahoma Glacier and along Emerald Ridge. The seismometers were set to capture signals from any debris flows that happened during the summer.
