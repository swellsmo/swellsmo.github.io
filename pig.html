Rift Evolution on the Pine Island Ice Shelf

This work is part of my undergraduate honors thesis for the Geosciences Major at Wellesley College

In this work, I identify three large rifts on Pine Island Glacier that form, propagate, and eventually calve off large, tabular icebergs between 2015 and 2020. I trace these rifts at significant time points, and sample the stresses present leading up to the rift and along the rift itself.

 

This work is still in preparation.
