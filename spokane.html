Rifting of the Supercontintent Rodinia

Spokane, WA - August, 2021

 

I spent a week in Eastern Washington with Dr. Athena Eyster as part of her field work on the rifting of the supercontinent Rodinia. We mapped outcrops and sketched them in our field notebooks, noting grain size and layer thickness. We found outcrops of metamorphic greenstone, suggesting volcanic activity in the area. Samples were collected to take back to Johns Hopkins University for dating.
