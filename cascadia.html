Cascadia 2021

Northwest Oregon - June, 2021

 

Through the University of Oregon, I spent two weeks in Northwest Oregon deploying Fairfield Seismic Nodes to help map the Cascadia Subduction Zone. The nodes were recording air shots from a ship off the Oregon Coast. My field partner and I successfully deployed and retreived 69 nodes stretching from Tillamook to Forest Park in Portland.
